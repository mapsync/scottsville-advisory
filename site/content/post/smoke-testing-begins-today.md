The Sanitary Sewer Evaluation Survey (SSES) is the first step in identifying the issues at Baptist Hollow.

Smoke will be emitting from manholes and different sources during the testing phase.  If you experience smoke coming into your building or house or have questions, contact the Scottsville Water and Gas Department at 270.237.4402.
