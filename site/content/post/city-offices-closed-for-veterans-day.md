Monday trash will be picked up on Tuesday, November 12, 2024, along with regular Tuesday customers. 

There will be no bus service on Monday. Regular route will resume on Tuesday. 

Scottsville City Council will meet in regular session on Tuesday, November 12, 2024, at 9 am in the council room located n the 2nd floor of the City-County Building.
