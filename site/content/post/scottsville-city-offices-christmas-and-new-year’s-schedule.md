description: >-
  City offices will be closed on Tuesday and Wednesday, December 24 and December
  25, in observance of Christmas. 


  City offices will be closed on Tuesday and Wednesday, December 31 and January 1 in observance of New Year’s. 


  There will be no bus service on these four days.
---
Tuesday, December 24, trash will be picked up on Thursday, December 26, along with regular Thursday customers. 

 Wednesday, December 25, trash will be picked up on Friday, December 27, along with regular Friday customers.  

Tuesday, December 31, trash will be picked up on Thursday, January 2, along with regular Thursday customers.

Wednesday, January 1, trash will be picked up on Friday, January 3, along with regular Friday customers.
