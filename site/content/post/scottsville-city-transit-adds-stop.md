The Scottsville City Transit Bus will begin stopping at the Allen County Health Department Monday, February 8 as a regular stop. 

Allen County Health Department will begin vaccinations on Monday, February 8 for those persons 70+ that have been on the waiting list and have been contacted by Health Department officials.
