  Scottsville City utilities customers with issues after normal business hours,
  8:00 am-4:30 pm, on holidays or during weekends, are to call Scottsville
  Dispatch at 270.237.3611.
---
The “on call” person will be notified of the issue and will make a site inspection. Give dispatcher your name, address and contact information. 

During normal business hours, contact Scottsville Water and Gas Department at 270.237.4402
