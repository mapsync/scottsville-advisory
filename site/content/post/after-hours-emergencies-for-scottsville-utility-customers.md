description: >
  Scottsville City utility customers with issues after normal business hours, on
  holidays, or during weekends, are to call Scottsville On-Call Dispatch at
  270-237-3611.
---
The “on call” person will be notified of the issue and will make a site inspection. Please give the dispatcher your name, address and contact information.

During our normal business hours (M-F, 8:00am-4:30pm), please contact Scottsville Water and Gas Department at 270-237-4402 to ask questions or report any issues with utility services.
