Halloween Trick-or-Treating will take place in Scottsville on Saturday, October 31, from 4 PM-7 PM.

Those that choose to participate are asked to adhere to social distancing guidelines, face covering recommendations and other guidelines stipulated by the local health department.

If residents are handing out candy, make sure you have an outside light on. For those who don't want to participate, do not leave an outside light on.
