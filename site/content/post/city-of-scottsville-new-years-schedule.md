Scottsville City Offices will be closed on Thursday, December 31 and Friday, January 1 for the New Year's holiday.



Thursday trash will be picked up on Monday, January 4 along with regular Monday customers.

Friday trash will be picked up on Tuesday, January 5 along with regular Tuesday customers.
