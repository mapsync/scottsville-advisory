The 2020 City Property Taxes were mailed out last week.  Be sure to pay by *December 15* to receive a 2% discount!  You can pay by cash, check or credit/debit card.

In Person:  City Hall Room #8 (Monday-Friday 8:00-4:30)

Dropbox:  Located outside City-County Building next to Fire Dept OR located by City Hall doors.

Mail payments to:  City of Scottsville, 201 W. Main St., Suite 8, Scottsville, KY 42164

Phone payment:  Call 270-237-4472 to pay by credit/debit card (no fee).
