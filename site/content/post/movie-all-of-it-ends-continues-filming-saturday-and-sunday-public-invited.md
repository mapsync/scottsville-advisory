Producer, Director George Demick will be in Scottsville on June 19 and 20, both daytime shoots, to continue filming his movie, "All of It Ends," a Sci-Fi Horror movie.

The filming will take place at the following locations:

Saturday, June 19; W. Main blocked from S. Dixon to W. Maple. S Bedelia and N. Bedelia blocked.

Sunday,  June 20;  Bucks Way blocked at S. Bedelia and S. Dixon.

George invites the public to watch the filming,  and if you're interested in being an extra or production assistant, show up at 9:30.
