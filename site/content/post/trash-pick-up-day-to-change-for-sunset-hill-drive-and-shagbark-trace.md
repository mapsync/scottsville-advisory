description: Starting on Wednesday, September 21, 2022, trash customers on
  Sunset Hill Dr and Shagbark Trace, will have their trash picked up on
  WEDNESDAY instead of Thursday.

Trash customers have been contacted in person or left a door hanger by Code Enforcement Officer, Jerry Biddle, informing them of the upcoming change in the day of pick up from Thursday to Wednesday.

T﻿his change was made to reduce the number of customers on the Thursday route and to provide better customer service and more cost efficiency to both routes.
