  I am posting three options available, at this time, for Allen County/Scottsville residents to get on the vaccination waiting list to be called for an appointment to receive the vaccine.
---
1    Allen County Health Department :

Allen County, Kentucky residents age 70 and above who would like to be scheduled for a Covid-19 vaccination can call the Health Department to be added to a waiting list. Please call 270.239.7378 Monday through Thursday between the hours 9:00 AM-12:00 PM and 1:00 PM-3:00 PM. Once vaccine is available, the Health Department will contact the resident to schedule an appointment. A driver's license or other government form of proof of address and age will be required at the time of the appointment.

2    Rural Health Clinic/Medical Center Scottsville

Call  270.618.3700 to be added to their waiting list. You will receive a call for more information when the vaccine becomes available.

3   Medical Center Bowling Green

Text "SENIOR" to 270.796.3200

You will receive response for DOB, Phone # and email address.

\*\*THESE OPTIONS ARE TO BE ADDED TO A WAITING LIST TO BE CONTACTED, NOT TO SCHEDULE VACCINE!
