---
title: City Property Tax Discount Until December 15
draft: false
date: 2025-12-04T18:09:26.653Z
description: Scottsville City property taxes can be paid until December 15 to
  receive 2% discount.
---
After December 15, property taxes can be paid until January 31, 2026 at face value. 

After January 31 until April 1, a 6% penalty will be added to property taxes paid. 

After April 1, a 19% penalty will be added to property tax bill.
