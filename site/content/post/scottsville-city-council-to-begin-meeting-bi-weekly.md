---
title: Scottsville City Council To Begin Meeting Bi-Weekly
draft: false
date: 2021-03-26T02:21:05.102Z
description: Scottsville City Council will meet on the 2nd and 4th Monday of
  each month beginning April 12, 2021.
---
The Scottsville City Council met in special session on Monday, March 22, 2021 and unanimously voted to amend the ordinance specifying meeting times.

Effective April 12, 2021, The Scottsville City Council will meet on the 2nd Monday of each month at 12:00 pm and the 4th Monday of each month at 6 pm. 

Council meetings will continue as Zoom teleconferences, but will now be considered regular session meetings.
