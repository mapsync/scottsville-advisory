Lauren Cherry will be sworn in as Scottsville City Clerk on Tuesday morning, April 19, at 8:00 am by Judge Thurmond in the Circuit Court Room in the Judicial Center.

City Offices will delay opening until 8:30, so employees can attend the ceremony.
