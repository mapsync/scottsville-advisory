If you detect a gas smell, observe water running above ground near a manhole or have water meter problems after business hours, Monday-Friday, 8 am-4:30 pm, call Dispatch at 270.237.3611 to report the issue and the “on call” person will be notified. 

During normal business hours, call 270.237.4402 or 270.237.3238.
