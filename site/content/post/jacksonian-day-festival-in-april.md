Jacksonian Day pageants wil be held in April, along with the Walk of Fame ceremony at 10 am on April 18.  Nominations for Hometown Hero are  being accepted by the Scottsville-Allen County Chamber of Commerce. For more information, call 270.237.4782 or visit the website www.scottsvilleky.info

The Scottsville Opry will still be held Friday and Saturday, September 18 and 19, for the 2026 season, but will move to April in 2027.
