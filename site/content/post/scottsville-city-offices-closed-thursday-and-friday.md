description: >-
  Scottsville City Offices will be closed Thursday and Friday, November 23 and
  24, in observance of Thanksgiving. 

  There will be no bus service on Thursday or Friday.

Thursday trash will be picked up on Monday, November 27, along with regular Monday customers. \
Friday trash will be picked up on Tuesday, November 28, along with regular Tuesday customers. 

City bus will resume normal route on Monday, November 27.
