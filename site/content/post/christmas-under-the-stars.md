Friday night, December 3,  the city of Scottsville will be part of the Heart of Scottsville's Christmas Under the Stars event, which will start at 6 PM with the official lighting of the City of Scottsville's Christmas Tree on the Square. Food vendors, carolers, handbells, in addition to Joyful Noise will be part of the festivities.  Broken Road will be playing from 7:30 pm-9 pm. Businesses will be opened extended hours for shopping!

Saturday, December 4, the annual Christmas Parade will start at 5 PM at the Walgreens Intersection. The parade route will be South Court, turning right onto East Main and will end at T & K Medical.  \*\*Note the parade procession will be moving in the opposite direction as it has from past parades due to logistical issues.

Everyone come out and enjoy the kick off to the Christmas season!!
