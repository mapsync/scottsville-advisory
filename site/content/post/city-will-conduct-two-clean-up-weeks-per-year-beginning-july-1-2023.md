A﻿s stated in the resolution, there will be no change in brush pick up. You can still call the Mayor's office at 270.237.3238 to get on the brush pick up list.

\*﻿Note the change in handling of mattresses during the two clean up weeks.

\*T﻿hese large items may still be taken to the Allen County-Scottsville Transfer Station by the owners to be disposed of at their own cost.\*
