The City of Scottsville has now made online billing available for utility payments! Create an account through the [online portal](http://Scottsvilleky.utilitydistrict.com) using your billing account number and last name.

Payments can still be made in-person in City Hall, or by phone number at (270) 237-4402 or (270) 237-3831
