Scottsville City Offices will be closed on Thursday, December 24 and Friday, December 25 for the Christmas holiday. 





Thursday trash will be picked up on Monday, December 28 along with regular Monday customers. 

Friday trash will be picked up on Tuesday, December 29 along with regular Tuesday customers.
