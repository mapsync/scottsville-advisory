Filming begins this Sunday on the movie "All of it Ends", a sci-fi horror film produced and directed by George Demick, who worked with Stephen King early in his career.

Location for Sunday's shoot will be West Maple blocked at S. Bedelia and S. Welch. S. Dixon blocked at Cline Alley and Bucks Way.

Set up begins at 9:30 am.

The public is invited to watch the movie being filmed.  If anyone is interested in being a production assistant or an extra in the film, contact George at george.demick@gmail.com. You can also show up that morning.

Future daytime filming dates: June 19, June 20, July 10.

Night filming will be July 17 and July 31.

It should be a fun experience seeing the filming of a movie here locally!!

I invite everyone to drop by!
