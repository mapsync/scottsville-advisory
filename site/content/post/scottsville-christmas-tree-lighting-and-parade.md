On Saturday, December 2, the Scottsville-Allen County Chamber of Commerce Christmas Parade will start at 4:30 PM at the intersection at Walgreens. 

The parade route will follow South Court up to the Square, turning right onto East Main Street. 

The parade will end in front of the Dollar General Warehouse.
