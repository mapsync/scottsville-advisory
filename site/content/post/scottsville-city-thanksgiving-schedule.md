Scottsville City Offices will be closed Thursday and Friday in observance of Thanksgiving.

 The City Bus will run on Monday and Wednesday this week 7 AM-4 PM and on Saturday from 8 AM-2 PM for Small Business Saturday.

Thursday trash will be picked up on Monday, November 30 and Friday trash will be picked up on Tuesday, December 1.  Monday and Tuesday customers will have normal pick up.
