Scottsville City Offices will be closed Thursday and Friday, November 26 and 27 in observance of Thanksgiving.

 The City Bus will run on Monday and Wednesday this week 7 AM-4 PM and on Saturday from 8 AM-2 PM for Small Business Saturday. Normal stops for residences and retail businesses.

Trash pick up for Thursday customers will be on Monday, November 30, and trash pick up for Friday customers will be on Tuesday, December 1.  Monday and Tuesday customers will have normal trash pick up.
