---
title: Use of Fireworks in the Scottsville City Limits
draft: false
date: 2022-06-23T19:57:28.892Z
description: Dates and times to ignite, fire or explode fireworks in the
  Scottsville City Limits were established by Ordinance  94.03 passed by the
  Scottsville City Council in 2021.
---
Fireworks, including consumer fireworks, may only be used between the hours of noon and 10 pm on June 27 through July 3 and on July 5 and between the hours of noon and 11 pm on July 4 of any year.

For more information on the fireworks ordinance for the City of Scottsville, go to the website, **cityofscottsville.org**
